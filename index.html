What is Persona Palette?

    A Comprehensive Styling Tool

  • Persona Palette analyzes your unique seasonal colors and body shape.
  • Provides tailored recommendations on clothing combinations, ideal metals, and flattering silhouettes
  • A data-driven approach to personal branding and professional presentation.

    12-Season Color Analysis System

  • Based on the Munsell color theory, which evaluates the hue, value, and chroma of different facial features.
  • Hue = whether your skin undertone is warm or cool.
  • Value = how light or dark your skin is.
  • Chroma/clarity = whether your face is more bright and clear or more soft and muted.

    Impactful Color Analysis

  • The goal of this analysis is to achieve a look where your clothing and complexion complement each other greatly.
  • Wearing your correct "season" can enhance your natural features, making you look more vibrant and youthful, and boost your confidence.
