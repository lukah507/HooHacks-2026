Welcome to
Persona Palette!

Persona Palette is a comprehensive styling tool that helps you look your best by analyzing your unique seasonal colors and body shape. The system provides tailored recommendations on clothing combinations, ideal metals, and flattering silhouettes while identifying specific styles to avoid. By integrating face shape and jewelry analysis, it offers a complete, data-driven approach to personal branding and professional presentation.
